Bank Management System:-
A Bank Management system is a system where all the details of the customers are
stored like customer_id, customer_name, account_type, customer_location,
contact_no, acc_opening_date, debit_card_issued(yes/no), debit_card_issued_on,
nominee_registered(yes/no), minimum_amount_in_account, multiple_acc(yes/no). Here
a small part of implementation has to be done which are as:-
Create a class called bank_mgmt with above attributes and store the database for 10
customers.
Specific task Group Member-1
1. Display all the customers who have ’Saving accounts’ as account type.
2. Display all the customers who have registered their nominees.
3. Display all the customers who were issued debit card today.
Specific task Group Member-2
4. Display all the customers whose names start with ‘M’.
5. Display all the details of the customers whose location is ‘Ahmedabad’
6. Display all the customers whose account opening date falls in this month.
Specific task Group Member-3
7. Display all the customers whose minimum account in the bank is less than 1000/-.
8. Display all the customers who have multiple accounts and whose names start
with ‘A’
